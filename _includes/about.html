The government tech marketplace is at an inflection point.


There are now hundreds of startups actively disrupting the $100B+ government technology ecosystem, working in thousands of jurisdictions, with the support of tens of millions of investment dollars.

Yet — for those of us working in the field — we know that we have truly just scratched the surface.

More support is needed for cities looking to leverage new technology; more investment is needed for entreprenuers building new companies; and more visibility is needed for every member of this ecosystem to encourage even more people to get in the game. That's why we're building EthosLabs.

Part consultancy, part accelerator, and part product shop, EthosLabs is a new initiative to drive forward the government technology ecosystem by working hand-in-hand with others doing the same: innovators within city hall, entreprenuers building startups, and investors seeking to catalyze change.

We're just getting started, and we'll share more about our strategy over time. For now, get in touch if you're interested, and let's start the conversation.
